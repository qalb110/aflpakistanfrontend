ASIAN CHAMPS TEAM PREVIEW

Big warm welcome to the Pakistan Markhors, who will be making their Asian Champs debut this year! The AFL Pakistan representative side have played in two AFL International Cups (2014 and 2017) and will bring a breath of fresh air in terms of cultural confidence to the Asian Champs with their passion and camaraderie. If their preparations are anything to go by expect the Pakistan Markhors to be a fit and physical side, who will embrace the heat of the battle. Keep an eye on elder statesman and inspirational leader Inam Ul Haq, who is a tough battering ram with a crunching tackle. Expect the Pakistan Markhors to become crowd favourites overnight and thus they shouldn't be taken lightly!
